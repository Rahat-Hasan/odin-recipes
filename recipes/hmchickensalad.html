Honey Mustard Chicken Salad


Overview

Prep: 20 mins

Additional: 2 hrs

Total: 1 hrs 20 mins

Servings: 4



Ingredient List



















Directions

Combine chicken, grapes, apples, celery, and walnuts in a large bowl. Combine mayonnaise, honey, and Dijon mustard in a small bowl, stirring well. Gently stir into chicken mixture. Cover and chill.



Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 3534 calories; protein 16.7g; carbohydrates 46g; fat 33.7g; cholesterol 47mg; sodium 485mg.
